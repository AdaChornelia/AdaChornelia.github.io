BATS and CAVE

1.1 Bats

Bats is flying mammals. Many people are misunderstood the existence of bats. Often people associated it with horror, spooky, unfortunate, bad luck etc. However, bats is just an animal, nothing related to ghost or spirit realms. Instead, they provide important role in ecosystem service and benefit to maintain healthy ecosystem

1.2 Cave

most of bats stay in caves, some of them living in tree holes, or abandon building, and even in people house when there is no available roosting sites. However, many people are unhappy with the existence of bats around their house due to believe in myth. Most of bats are insectivorous and mostly insectivorous bats roosts in the cave. However, some of fruits bats also living in the caves. Therefore, cave is one of the important sites to maintain bats population
